Our Love Story

It started in 2023

He thought she looked and sounded pretty ﬁne.

She ignored him for a while - "I was busyyyyyy - Tracey".

But she came around and they bonded over messages about garden centres, a shared love of skiing (hmmmm) and home Improvements (in hindsight not great chat).

A ﬁrst date in SOHO at a sushi restaurant, a ﬁrst kiss in Piccadilly station and a ﬁrst weekend away in Bath all sealed the deal.

Being welcomed by loving and welcoming families and sharing lots of laughter together set us on a path to today.

We’ve discovered the delights of Wallington and Alperton, and been on some adventures too, from Belgium to Bangkok, Miami to New York and much in between.

In Koh Samui set amongst palm trees on a sandy beach one evening in March 2025, we were engaged.

And now for the wedding, a time to celebrate with the family and friends that have been with us throughout our journey and who will be for the future.
